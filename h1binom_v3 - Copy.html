📊 H1 Maths: Binomial Distribution

Exam Logic: Inequality Cheat Sheet

• "At most 5" → P(X ≤ 5) → binomcdf(n, p, 5)
• "Fewer than 5" → P(X ≤ 4) → binomcdf(n, p, 4)
• "At least 5" → P(X ≥ 5) → 1 - binomcdf(n, p, 4)
• "More than 5" → P(X ≥ 6) → 1 - binomcdf(n, p, 5)
• "Between 5 and 8 (inclusive)" → binomcdf(n, p, 8) - binomcdf(n, p, 4)
